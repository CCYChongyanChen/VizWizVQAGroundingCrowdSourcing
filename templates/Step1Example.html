Select "No" when just one question is asked (Example 1 & Example 2).
Select "Yes" when more than one question is asked (Example 3 & Example 4).


Exception: Select "No" when the question just repeats itself or only one question needs to be answered (Example 2).

Question: Is this lactose free?
Answer: Yes.

Example 1: No

Explanation: Just one question is asked.

Question: "Could you tell me what is this? Thank you. What is this?"
Answer: HEB Lactose free milk.

Example 2: No

Explanation: Though there are two question marks, the question conveys the same idea. Hence, only one question needs to be answered.

Question: Could you tell me what color is the chair? Where is the key?
Answer: Black. Table.

Example 3: Yes

Explanation: More than one question is asked.

Question: Is this a chair what color?
Answer: Yes. Black.

Example 4: Yes

Explanation: Though there is just one question mark, more than one question is asked.
